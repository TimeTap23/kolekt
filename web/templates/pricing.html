Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI credits?

AI credits are used for content generation, hashtag suggestions, and other AI-powered features. Each AI generation costs 1 credit.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do credits roll over?

Monthly credits reset each billing cycle. Purchased credit packs never expire and can be used anytime.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can purchase additional credit packs anytime. Your account will continue to work for posting and scheduling, just not for AI features.
